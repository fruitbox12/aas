# Performance Cards

<br />

## Uses

The Performance Cards translate the price of ZAP, Total bonded Dot's to all Oracles, and the amount of ZAP bonded to all Oracles.

## Terminology

**PRICE ZAP/USDT**: Displays the current price of ZAP.

**TOTAL BONDED**: Displays the amount of DOT's bonded to all Oracles listed in the Marketplace.

**TOTAL VOLUME**: Displays the amount of USD circulating in the Marketplace.
