# FAQ

<br />

## Uses

On the bottom of the home page containing Frequently Asked Questions from the community.

## Implementation

Mapping the `FAQS_TEXT` constant to easily edit the FAQ section.
Mapping the`SOCIAL` constant for the links to social media profiles.

## Expected Behavior

All cards start as collapsed.
Expanding the cards shows the answers.
